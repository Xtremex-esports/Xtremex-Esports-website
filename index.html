About

Support

Frequently Asked Question

How can I join XTremeX Esports?
XTremeX scouts and recruits talented players based on zeal, skills, teamwork, and competitive experience. The team also develops promising talent when it sees strong potential.
What games does XTremeX Esports compete in?
XTremeX publicly highlights activity across EAFC, eFootball, NBA 2K, EA UFC, PUBG Mobile, Call of Duty Mobile, MLBB, Dota 2, Valorant, Tekken, and Street Fighter.
Do you offer training programs for aspiring esports players?
Yes. Coaching, mentorship, and structured training programs are part of the team’s approach to improving gameplay, strategy, and teamwork.
How can we become a sponsor or partner with your team?
XTremeX offers sponsorship and partnership opportunities including brand placement, event collaborations, and custom campaigns. The direct contact point is faosolutions@gmail.com.
Where can I watch your team compete?
The team points supporters to YouTube, Facebook, and its social channels for match schedules, live content, and appearance updates.

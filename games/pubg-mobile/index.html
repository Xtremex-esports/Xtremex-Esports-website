PUBG Mobile Sector

PUBG Mobile

XTremeX PUBG Mobile carries one of the hardest histories inside the wider project: ambition, real international representation, sabotage, and now a determined rebuild toward unfinished work.

How It Started

Origin The PUBG side began because Imperial, known as master of the game, wanted to prove he was even more optimal on PUBG than on CODM.
Build Phase That challenge turned into active recruitment, real competition, and a squad that fought through a lot of ups and downs.
National Duty The team was graced with the chance to represent Ghana, which remains one of the proudest landmarks in the sector's history.

The Story So Far

  • Early Rise What started as a challenge quickly became a real PUBG Mobile project. The squad kept playing, recruiting, and pushing through rough phases until it became strong enough to carry Ghana into international competition.
  • IESF Country Friendly One of the biggest achievements came when the team finished 7th out of 16 African countries in an IESF country friendly tournament. Representing Ghana there gave the PUBG side a real continental moment.
  • Sabotage And Collapse The original team did not simply fade. It was hit by sabotage again and again, especially around tournaments, and that constant interference damaged progress, chemistry, and momentum.
  • Cheated Match In one IESF tournament run, the squad faced a team that used hacks. Evidence was gathered and the matter was reported, but the organizers did not take action because the accused side was linked to one of the organizers. That left a deep scar on the sector.
  • Rulebreakers The PUBG side even formed a smurf team called Rulebreakers because GPOW was being sabotaged so often in tournaments. That alone says a lot about how hostile the environment had become.
  • Confidence Damage Matters got worse when the EAG President would post whenever the team lost. That repeated public pressure weakened player confidence and added to the mental damage the squad was already carrying.
  • Current Mission Now the sector is rebuilding and trying to pick up from where it left off, not as a team pretending the past never happened, but as one determined to recover the ground it earned before things were torn apart.

Rebuild Position

The PUBG Mobile page now stands as the place to document that return properly: the rebuilt squad, the names carrying the next phase, the tournament record that follows, and the proof that the sector still has life after everything that tried to break it.
